---
title: 'Email Member Link to Membership'
description: 'Send members a link to view and manage their membership'
---

Send a member an email with a link to view their membership. The link will prompt them to login or create an account if they don't have one yet.

## Send Link Instructions

1. Login to [mychaiclub.com](https://mychaiclub.com) and navigate to **Members** in the top menu
2. Select a member
3. Open the edit menu and click **Send email to connect account**
4. Click **Send Email**
