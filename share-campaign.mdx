Your campaign page can be shared as a hosted page or embedded on your existing website (you can use both).

## Hosted Page

Use the hosted URL to share your campaign directly.

### Customize Your URL

1. Login to [mychaiclub.com](https://mychaiclub.com) and navigate to **Settings** in the top menu
2. Under the **Share Campaign** section, click **Edit Slug**
3. Enter your preferred URL slug (e.g., your organization name)
4. Click **Save**

## Embed on Your Website

Embed the campaign page directly on your existing website. See the [Embed Campaign Page](/embed-campaign) instructions.
