title: 'Campaign Page Content'
description: 'Customize your campaign page to engage donors'
---

Customize your campaign page content in the **Settings** page on [mychaiclub.com](https://mychaiclub.com). See our [sample campaign](https://mychaiclub.com) for inspiration.

## Logo

Upload your logo without a background for best results.

## Campaign Name

Describe what you're fundraising for.

- Sunshine Valley Chai Partners
- Chabad Sunshine Valley Supporter Club

## Button Text

Match your button text to your campaign.

- Join Now
- Partner with Us
- Become a Monthly Partner

## Call to Action

Motivational text donors see first. Focus on:
- Impact of their donation
- Organization highlights
- Your goal or promise

**Tip:** Center text, add color to highlights, and use headers.

## Campaign Goal (Optional)

Set a fundraising target as a dollar amount or member count.

## About Campaign

Tell your story and explain why you're fundraising.

**Tip:** Use multiple paragraphs, center text, add color, and include headers.

## Gallery Images

Showcase your work with images that demonstrate your impact.
