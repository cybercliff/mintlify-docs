## Basic Setup

1. Go to [mychaiclub.com](https://mychaiclub.com) and click **Get Started**
2. Login or create an account
3. Fill out your basic information
4. Select a billing plan
5. Add a payment processor
6. Your campaign is now active and ready to accept members

## Share Your Campaign

Decide how you want to share your campaign (you can use both):

- **Hosted page** - Use the MyChaiClub URL (e.g., mychaiclub.com/jewishsv)
- **Embed on your website** - Add the campaign to your existing site

See [Share Your Campaign](/share-campaign) for detailed instructions.
