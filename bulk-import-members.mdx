description: 'Import existing recurring donors into your campaign'
---

Import existing members if you have recurring donors from before using MyChaiClub.

## Import Instructions

1. Login to [mychaiclub.com](https://mychaiclub.com) and navigate to **Members** in the menu
2. Click the **Import** button on the top right
3. Follow the instructions in the popup modal
4. Download the sample sheet
5. Add your existing members to the sheet
6. Upload the completed sheet
7. You'll receive an email notification once the import is complete

<Note>
Members will be imported without a payment method. Payment methods can be added later.
</Note>
